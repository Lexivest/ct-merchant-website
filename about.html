CTM

Institutional Profile

CT-Merchant Ltd. (CTM) is a specialized information technology firm providing critical data infrastructure for Nigeria’s local trade ecosystems. We facilitate the digital translation of physical inventory and merchant locations, providing a structured ecosystem for consumer exploration.

Our operational framework prioritizes Data Accuracy and Discovery Optimization, reducing friction in local commerce while preserving the traditional merchant–customer relationship.

CTM operates strictly as a neutral information repository. We remain independent of financial settlements and logistics to maintain data integrity and search excellence.
