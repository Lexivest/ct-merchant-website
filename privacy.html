Privacy Policy

Data Protection Commitment

This policy explains how CTMerchant collects, uses, and protects personal information in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and global app store guidelines.

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

CTMerchant (“we”, “our”) operates a digital repository that lists physical shops, products, and locations for discovery and informational purposes only. This Privacy Policy describes how personal data is handled when you use our web and mobile platforms.

2. Information We Collect

We collect limited information necessary to operate and improve the platform securely, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and authentication tokens (e.g., via Google Sign-In).
  • Business Data: Shop listings, physical addresses, coordinates, and product details voluntarily provided by merchants.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, device types, and usage statistics (utilized strictly for security profiling and preventing platform abuse).
  • AI Interactions: Non-personally identifiable queries sent to our AI Assistant to improve product discovery.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide, secure, and maintain the digital repository.
  • Display accurate shop listings, product information, and map locations.
  • Enable direct communication between users and listed shops via WhatsApp or phone.
  • Enforce community guidelines and block malicious IP addresses.

Important: CTMerchant does not use personal data for payment processing, credit assessments, or financial transactions.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

CTMerchant utilizes local storage technologies (often referred to as caching) to dramatically improve platform performance and reduce data consumption for our users. We also use local storage to monitor free-tier usage of our AI Assistant. These caches do not track your activity across other websites.

5. Data Sharing

CTMerchant does not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only under the following circumstances:

  • When users voluntarily initiate contact (e.g., clicking to WhatsApp) with listed shops.
  • With highly secure, trusted cloud infrastructure providers (e.g., Supabase) strictly for platform hosting.
  • When formally required by law enforcement or valid legal requests.

6. Data Security & Account Deletion

Security: All data is securely hosted on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with Row-Level Security (RLS) and advanced encryption protocols. Access to personal and merchant data is strictly limited.

Account Deletion (Your Right to Erasure): You may request the complete deletion of your account and associated data at any time. To do so, please contact our support team at support@ct-merchant.com.ng. Upon receiving a verified request, we will erase your personal data from our active databases within 30 days.

7. Your Privacy Rights

In compliance with data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data (as outlined above).
  • Restrict or object to certain types of processing.

Questions regarding your privacy?

Our compliance team is ready to assist you with any clarifications or data requests.
