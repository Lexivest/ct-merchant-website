Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

This policy explains how CT-MERCHANT LTD. collects, uses, and protects personal information.

1. Introduction

CT-MERCHANT LTD. (“CTMERCHANT”, “we”, “our”) operates a digital repository that lists physical shops, products, and locations for discovery and informational purposes only. This Privacy Policy describes how personal data is handled when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect limited information necessary to operate and improve the platform, including:

  • Account information such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Business listing details voluntarily provided by shop owners.
  • General location data (city or area) to support local discovery.
  • Technical data such as device type and usage statistics.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the digital repository.
  • Display shop listings, product information, and locations.
  • Enable direct communication between users and listed shops.
  • Improve platform performance and usability.

CTMERCHANT does not use personal data for payments, credit assessment, or transaction processing.

4. Data Sharing

CTMERCHANT does not sell personal data. Information may be shared only:

  • When users voluntarily share contact details with listed shops.
  • With trusted service providers strictly for platform operations.
  • When required by law or lawful government request.

CTMERCHANT does not share data with tax authorities or third parties for enforcement purposes.

5. Data Security & Storage

CTMERCHANT implements industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and ensure data integrity:

  • Cloud Security: All data is securely hosted on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with advanced encryption protocols.
  • Secure Transmission: We use secure communication protocols (HTTPS/SSL) to protect data transmitted over the internet.
  • Restricted Access: Access to personal and merchant data is strictly limited to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.
  • Continuous Monitoring: We regularly review and update our security practices to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or misuse of your data.

Questions?
Contact us if you need clarification regarding this Privacy Policy via our Support channels.
